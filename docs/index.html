About Me

Technology and agronomy working together

I am an agronomist graduated from the National University Toribio Rodríguez de Mendoza de Amazonas (UNTRM). I have experience in scientific research, data analysis, and field data collection. I use R for statistical analysis and have developed digital tools for research, such as the HUITO system for label generation.

I am interested in agricultural production, fieldwork, and applied research in agrarian systems. I am currently strengthening my skills in programming, digital tools, and data analysis to apply them in agronomy and improve decision-making in the agricultural sector.
